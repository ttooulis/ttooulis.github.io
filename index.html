Research Interests

My research interests generally fall in the field of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and specifically on how AI and humans can work together. I'm especially interested in areas like knowledge representation, reasoning, interpretability, contestability and natural language understanding. My research aims to solve real-world problems, improving how people interact in natural language with AI systems. This includes helping people question AI decisions and ensuring these systems comply with regulations, norms and values.

One track of my work is on natural language translation into symbolic form and the application of coaching paradigm on the translation process of natural language advice.

Another track of my research is related to contestable dialogues: i.e. how to enable humans to contest and dispute decisions taken by black-box AI systems. My goal is to propose a detailed architecture of a system as a solution to a real-world problem.
